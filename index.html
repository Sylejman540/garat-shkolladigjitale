The Voice Helper

Voice Helper is a stand-alone web application that will work across all of your technology. After teaching Voice Helper your unique speech, there are three key modes: Speak, Dictate, and Integrations.

Speak feature enables speech-to-speech communication, perfect for interacting with others or managing smart home systems. Simply click "Start Speaking" and say a phrase, such as "Can I have a cup of tea?". Then tap "Stop Listening" to let Voice Companion convey your message using a clear computerized voice. You can personalize the voice settings via the menu. Additionally, Speak integrates with ChatGPT, allowing you to ask questions or initiate conversations directly.

Dictate converts speech into text, making it ideal for writing emails, taking notes, or drafting messages. You can use voice commands for hands-free editing or manually adjust text as needed.
